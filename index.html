Welcome to IEEE RAS CBIT

Our Mission

To foster the development and facilitate the exchange of scientific and technical knowledge in Robotics and Automation that benefits members, the profession and humanity

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most respected global organization in Robotics and Automation. We strive to advance in innovation, education, and fundamental and applied research in robotics and automation

About IEEE RAS CBIT


IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) Student Chapter of Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), initiated by the Departments of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Information Technology and Mechanical Engineering, works to contribute towards evolution and exchange of scientific knowledge on robotics and automation with applied theoretical, practical knowledge. The Society has immensely contributed in boosting the student members towards robotics and automation, being a great source of professional, scientific, and technical information. We look forward to boosting the local soft and hard skills through organizing events and competitions

Our Happenings

Types of Events we conduct

Workshops

Our Workshops give an opportunity for participants to have interaction with distinguished speakers and trainers. The sessions focus mainly on practical, hands-on training

Seminars

Seminars give an opportunity to discuss topics in depth. These sessions prioritize overall comprehending and learning of the theoretical knowledge given by skilled teachers or professionals.

Masterclass

Masterclasses are known to bring in high quality speakers with varied experiences. The sessions focus on a variety of skill improvement in both technical and theoretical aspects
