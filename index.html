Our Mission

To foster the development and facilitate the exchange of scientific and technical knowledge in Robotics and Automation that benefits members, the profession and humanity

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most respected global organization in Robotics and Automation. We strive to advance in innovation, education, and fundamental and applied research in robotics and automation

About IEEE RAS CBIT

IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) Student Chapter of Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), initiated by the Departments of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Information Technology and Mechanical Engineering of CBIT, works to contribute towards the evolution and exchange of scientific knowledge on robotics and automation with applied and theoretical and practical knowledge.
The field of robotics plays a major role in space exploration, human services, manufacturing, and many more fields such as intelligent machines and systems along with machine learning and artificial intelligence which has drastically caused a huge development recently in science and technology. The automation methods take place in various areas such as, different applications in industry, transportation, workplaces, and houses, ensuring better performance with increased efficiency and productivity. Robotics focuses on systems incorporating sensors and actuators that operate autonomously or semi-autonomously in cooperation with humans.
